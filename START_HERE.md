## Step 8: Common First Week Challenges

### Challenge 1: "I don't understand syntax errors"
**Solution:** Read error messages carefully. Google: "Python [error name]". Every programmer faces this - it gets easier!

### Challenge 2: "I'm falling behind the schedule"
**Solution:** Don't stress! Quality > speed. Take an extra day if needed. The timeline is a guide, not a law.

### Challenge 3: "Videos are boring, I want to code"
**Solution:** Great! Code along with videos (active learning). Pause frequently to try yourself.

### Challenge 4: "I forgot what I learned yesterday"
**Solution:** Normal! Use spaced repetition. Review notes daily. Teach someone (even your pet) what you learned.

### Challenge 5: "I feel overwhelmed"
**Solution:** Break it down. Focus only on today's tasks. Don't think about Month 6 yet. One day at a time.
